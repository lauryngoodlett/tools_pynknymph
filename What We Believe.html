What we
believe.

The beliefs underneath everything we do.

A manifesto, not a memo Everything traces back here
01

Body hair is normal.

You don't need to earn care by looking a certain way first. You don't need to apologize for your hair, your skin, your stretch marks, your scars, or your body. That's what we're here for.

02

Everybody deserves care.

Not eventually. Not after they lose weight or become more confident or "fix" something. Now.

03

Comfort shouldn't be a luxury.

A waxing appointment should not require bravery. People deserve to feel welcome, informed, respected, and comfortable from the moment they walk in.

04

Confidence isn't something we sell.

We don't give people confidence. We help create the conditions for it. The confidence was already theirs.

05

Education is part of the service.

People make better decisions when they understand their bodies. Questions are welcome. Curiosity is welcome. Nobody should leave feeling embarrassed for asking.

06

We take the work seriously. We don't take ourselves seriously.

Waxing involves vulnerable moments. Humor helps. Laughter helps. Being human helps.

07

Richmond matters.

Pynk Nymph exists because of the people who support it. We aren't trying to feel like a chain. We're trying to feel like home.

✦ ◆ The final belief · Why we exist

We help people feel comfortable in their bodies.

Not eventually. Not when they lose weight. Not when they become more confident. Not when they finally feel “ready.” Now.

We just happen to remove hair.
