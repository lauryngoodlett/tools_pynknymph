Logo lockups.

Hand-drawn liquid letterforms in two builds: cream-fill / aloe-outline for dark and accent surfaces, and the inverse aloe-fill / cream-outline for white and light surfaces. Pick the build that gives the cleanest contrast. Use the art in /assets directly — never recreate it.
