Principles.

Three rules that keep the brand honest. They show up in copy, color, type, and behavior — every time.

01 / Principle

Inclusive by default.

Pynk Nymph is for every body. Photography, language, and visuals reflect a wide range of identities — never an idealized fraction.

02 / Principle

Bold, with care.

Confident, expressive, and a little mischievous — but the warmth and craft underneath always lead. Personality without volume for its own sake.

03 / Principle

Clarity wins.

If a line is hard to read, it's wrong. We choose plain words, generous space, and high contrast — every time.

Typography.

Expressive serif. Grounded sans. Structural mono. Three jobs.

Pynk Nymph · Brand Guidelines · 202614 / 23

Type system.

Fraunces sets the tone. DM Mono carries the brand. DM Sans does the heavy lifting where it has to read.

Use this for · Headlines & big moments

Fraunces.

Use for big text only — post headlines, taglines, hero moments. Never for paragraphs or small text.

Aa
Use this for · Labels, captions & brand accents

DM Mono

Use for short structural text — pillar labels, post captions, callouts, slide numbers. This is what makes it feel like Pynk Nymph.

Aa
Use this for · Body text & documents

DM Sans

Use when readability matters most — long captions, emails, policy text, anything people actually read line by line.

Aa
When in doubt Fraunces for the hook · DM Mono for the label · DM Sans for the explanation.

04 / Typography · Hierarchy16 / 23

Do's & Don'ts.

If a line is hard to read, it's wrong. Lead with clarity — always.

✓Do

  • Use display type at large, airy sizes.Let Fraunces breathe — it's not for paragraphs.
  • Treat DM Mono as a brand accent.Labels, callouts, structural details — it's a vibe.
  • Keep DM Sans clean and consistent.Same size, same weight, comfortable line height.
  • Build hierarchy with scale, not decoration.Bigger before bolder. Color before italic.

×Avoid

  • Long paragraphs in Fraunces.Display type is not body type. Keep it to one line.
  • ALL CAPS in DM Sans body.Caps belong to DM Mono accents, not running text.
  • Mixing weights or sizes in one block.Pick one and commit. Visual noise reads as panic.
  • Italic for emphasis in body copy.Use weight, color, or scale. Not italic.
